WHEEL DATA RECORDING SYSTEM

The Wheel Data Recording System (WDRS) is a pioneering web application developed in-house, utilizing Bootstrap, PHP, and Microsoft SQL Server database technologies. Tailored specifically for the Indian Railways, WDRS revolutionizes the management of wheel set overhauling processes, encompassing both Intermediate Overhauling (IOH) and Periodic Overhauling (POH) procedures. With its modular architecture and comprehensive feature set, WDRS sets a new standard for efficiency, transparency, and data-driven decision-making in railway maintenance operations.

This system is designed to streamline the operations involved in handling wheel sets, from initial assessment to final assembly. With a focus on recording crucial details and guiding users through various stages, it ensures comprehensive oversight and efficient decision-making. From identifying unique components to directing them through specialized procedures like oxy cutting, press out, and wheel lathe, every step is seamlessly integrated into a cohesive workflow. By leveraging technology to enhance accuracy and accessibility, this system aims to optimize resource utilization and uphold safety standards in the maintenance of wheel sets.
